DEAP (Data Extraction and Analysis Program) is a modular package for the interactive display and editing of scientific data with the goal of producing publication quality plots. For more of an overview please see our about page and our FAQ.

DEAP is licensed under the GPL, and we are happy to have you use it as you will within the terms of that license, however, DEAP is built on PGPLOT which is not GPL. PGPLOT is free for non-commercial use but is strictly copyrighted by Caltech, so please do take care not to violate their license.
